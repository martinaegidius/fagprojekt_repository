Registration
************
Please register your software today, to ensure that you qualify for free customer support by telephone, fax or e-mail. 

You can register by
* sending back the relevant portion of the enclosed registration card to your local office
* sending an e-mail to register@cherwell.com providing all the information requested in the enclosed registration cards, including your product serial number.

* Visiting our home pages on http://www.cherwell.com and 
finding the Software Registration page under the Customer Service page.

EasyPlot User Mailing List
**************************

EasyPlot users now have their own email discussion forum.  To susbscribe, send an email message to majordomo@cherwell.com with the following message:

SUBSCRIBE EASYPLOT-USER
END

You will first receive an acknowledgement message.  Subsequently, you will receive all posts to easyplot-user and you can send your own messages to other 
subscribed users at easyplot-user@cherwell.com

We hope you will participate and help make this an active and useful forum.  

Support
*******
Please contact your local office for support, remembering to quote your registration number (found on the manual cover or on your portion of the registration card). Please also provide us with information about the system you are using and any special circumstances under which your 
problem occurs.

It is also advantageous to have your computer up and running in front of you when you call, so that our support team can take you through any step-by-step instructions.

You can look at our web site: http://www.cherwell.com for
